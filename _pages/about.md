I'm an Assistant Professor at the [Naveen Jindal School of Management](https://jindal.utdallas.edu) at the [University of Texas at Dallas](https://www.utdallas.edu).

I completed my PhD in 2020 at the [Graduate School of Business](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu), [Stanford](https://www.stanford.edu) ([OIT-group](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/faculty-research/faculty/academic-areas/operations-information-technology)), advised by [Daniela Saban](https://stanford.edu/~dsaban/).

I'm currently working on projects related to **matching**, **procurement**, **online platforms** and **behavioral operations**.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on:

- **Matching Markets**: School choice, college admissions, dating platforms
- **Online Platforms**: Assortment optimization, two-sided markets
- **Behavioral Operations**: Understanding how behavioral factors affect operational decisions
- **Procurement**: Transparency and peer effects in organizational purchasing
